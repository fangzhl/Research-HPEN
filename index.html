Welcome to the Function-oriented advanced manufacturing lab: FOAM-lab

We are interested in the process design and function control in the advanced manufacutring process on functional material, including:

Advanced manufacturing

・Electrical steel
・Ferromagnetic amorphous alloy
・Ferromagnetic nanocrystalline
・Martensitic stainless steel

Surface integrity

・Grain condition at subsurface
・Machining induced crystallization/dynamic recrystallization
・Grain deformation and refinement
・phase transition

Green manufacturing

・High-pressure coolant cutting
・Bioinspired microtextured cutter
・Computational fluid dynamics
・Forced cooling process

Research philosophy

"Everything should be made as simple as possible, but not simpler"

- Albert Einstein



With the rapid development of the Chinese society, the manufacturing industry is undergoing a tremendous change, requiring a role-transition of the engineers from “how to do” to “what to do”. I wish to enable people to become a better thinkers/designers/engineers to create new angles of engineering and technologies.

My research focuses on the advanced manufacturing field, valuing surface function performance as the ultimate objective to design/assess/fabricate the manufacturing system. Specifically, we extract scientific problems from the actual industrial application that can provide the transferrable knowledge to level the fundamental material processing.

We create concepts to simplify problems, so as to better understand the intrinsic nature behind them, enabling the proposal of creative ideas to reach the essential solution. This includes research on new mechanisms, phenomenon, approaches, observations.

The impact of our work can be imposed to where the transmission/drive units are made, as well as other industrial sectors.
