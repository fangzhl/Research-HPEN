Function-orientated advanced manufacturing



FOAM-lab focuses on the advanced manufacturing, surface engineering and sustainable green manufacturing on functional material to improve the on-site performance for the key parts of transmission/drive unit.

Kinematics (e.g. gear skiving, virtual prototyping)
Metallurgy (e.g. cutting-induced grain deformation, phase transition)
Function (e.g. magnetic, mechanical enhancement)

FOAM projects center the industrial practice for advancing the material process with high quality, productivity, safety and environmental protection.

Research interest-1：Transmission unit

Topics：

Power skiving technology

Hardened gear manufacturing

Surface integrity、Sustainable manufacturing



Open for Undergraduate and Graduate students

Topics：

Soft ferromagnetic material manufacturing
